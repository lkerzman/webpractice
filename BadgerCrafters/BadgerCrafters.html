We are a student organization at UW Madison. Badger crafters started in 2013 as a fun outlet for students to relax and have fun and create something awesome. We pride ourselves on using recycled materials to both maintain low cost for our members and to create sustainably. We have membership dues of $10 for a semester of $15 dollars for the year. On our website you can look through future events, view past crafts, suggest or vote for future crafts, and learn how to get in touch with us. ß
